Crystal Collector

The rules for the game:

  • You will be given a random number in the box at the start of the game.
  • Each of the four crystals displayed below has a different point value.
  • Your objective is to click a combination of the following crystals to equal the number given to you in the box.
  • If you click the correct combination of crystals and the total score equals the number in the box then, congratulations you win!
  • If the total value of the crystals clicked goes over the number given then, sorry you lose!
  • Good Luck!
